About Orbit Solutions

Orbit Solutions Ltd was established in 1998 and has grown tremendously over the years. With the name change and re-branding in the year 2005, Orbit Solutions Ltd became and is the leading Importer, distributor and provider of reliable and effective security equipment in the region. Due to the increased trust and confidence gained from a variety of customers with the growing need of latest technical security equipment, Orbit Solutions Ltd extended its offices in the whole region of Central and East Africa networking from Kenya to Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, Rwandan and DR Congo supplying genuine Security equipment Orbit Solutions Ltd was established in Kigali in the year 2014 and is the leading provider of electronic security equipment in Rwanda.
