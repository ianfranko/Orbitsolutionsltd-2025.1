About Us

Orbit Solutions Ltd is the Leading Importer, distributor and provider of reliable and effective security equipment in Central & East Africa Region (Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, Rwandan and DR Congo) due to the increased trust and confidence gained from a variety of customers with the growing need of latest technical security solutions.

Vision

To be the preferred Security and Safety Technology Solutions Provider with a standard one-stop-center at all times in the region.

Mission

To provide advanced and latest Security and Safety Technology Solutions that exceeds customers’ expectations and meeting industry standards
